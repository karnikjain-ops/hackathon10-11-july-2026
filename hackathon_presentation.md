## Slide 2: The Problem
**Current Emergency System Bottlenecks**
- **Delayed Dispatch:** Significant lag between an emergency call and ambulance dispatch.
- **Lack of Coordination:** Communication gap between the patient, ambulance drivers, and hospitals.
- **Inadequate Preparation:** Hospitals often receive patients without prior knowledge of their critical condition.
- **Resource Mismanagement:** Ambulances sent to hospitals with no available beds or specific specialized care.

---

## Slide 3: Our Solution
**An Integrated Healthcare SOS & Dispatch System**
- **One-Tap SOS App:** Patients can trigger an emergency with a single tap, sending live GPS and medical profiles.
- **Smart Dispatch:** Automatically identifies the nearest available hospital and dispatches an ambulance.
- **Real-Time Tracking:** Both patient and hospital can track the ambulance in real-time.
- **Instant AI First-Aid:** Provides critical "while you wait" first aid instructions based on symptoms.

---

## Slide 4: Key Features & Personas
**A Multi-Faceted Platform**
1. **Patient App (SOS):** Medical profile setup (Blood type, allergies), instant SOS, live status tracking.
2. **Hospital Dashboard:** Live alert of incoming patients, preparation time, and capacity management.
3. **Ambulance Dashboard:** GPS routing, patient details, and direct contact with the hospital.
4. **Admin Dashboard:** Oversees the entire ecosystem, manages fleet, and monitors response times.

## Slide 6: How It Works (User Journey)
1. **Trigger:** Patient hits SOS & types symptoms (e.g., "Severe Chest Pain").
2. **Triaging:** System logs GPS & profile, sends instant first aid tips.
3. **Dispatch:** Algorithm pings nearest available hospital and assigns a nearby ambulance.
4. **En-Route:** Ambulance driver gets the route; hospital prepares the emergency ward.
5. **Resolution:** Patient is admitted smoothly with zero time wasted on paperwork.
